🔐 Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 5, 2025

At Focusly, we care about your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect your information.

📊 What We Collect

  • Chat messages (for syncing across rooms)
  • Anonymous room IDs and user IDs (randomly generated)
  • Task and whiteboard content (if used)

🔍 How We Use It

  • To keep study rooms in sync for all users
  • To improve your experience in real-time collaboration
  • To maintain the whiteboard and chat functionality

🔒 Your Data

  • We do NOT sell or share your data with third parties.
  • All data is stored securely on Firebase (Google Cloud).
  • You can leave any room at any time, and your session data will be removed.

👶 Children’s Privacy

Focusly is not intended for children under 13. If you're under 13, please do not use the app.
