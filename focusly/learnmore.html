✨ Why Focusly?

Focusly isn’t just a productivity app. It’s your virtual study buddy, accountability partner, and focus enhancer — all in one.

📚 Real-Time Study Rooms

Join or create live study rooms where students from around the world gather to focus together. See who’s online, collaborate, and stay motivated.

🧠 Collaborative Pomodoro Timers

Sync your study sessions using proven techniques like Pomodoro — but with others. Everyone stays on track, together.

🎯 Track Progress & Build Streaks

Track your daily goals, focus time, and study consistency. Build a streak and get insights into your weekly and monthly performance.

🤝 Accountability & Social Motivation

Never study alone again. Connect with students who share your goals, create accountability circles, and make studying more social (but focused).

🎉 Gamified Experience

Earn XP, level up, collect badges, and climb the leaderboard. Studying doesn’t have to be boring when it feels like a game.

🔒 Privacy-First & Student-Friendly

No ads, no data selling. Focusly is designed with students in mind — lightweight, respectful, and effective.
