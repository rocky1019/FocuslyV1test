Focusly

What is Focusly?

Focusly is a real-time study web app that helps students stay focused, collaborate with peers, and make learning more fun and productive. With shared study rooms, live chat, Pomodoro timers, and a powerful whiteboard — Focusly creates the ultimate digital library experience.

Why we built this

Most students struggle with procrastination and distractions while studying alone. Focusly was created to make studying feel less lonely and more interactive — so you can work together, track your progress, and build strong study habits in a shared space.

Built by Synth Studio

Synth Studio is a small app and web development branch focused on building fun, useful, and creative tools for the modern digital world. Focusly is one of our proudest experiments — blending productivity with collaboration.

Built using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Firebase.
